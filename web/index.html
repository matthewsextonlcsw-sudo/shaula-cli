The honesty gate is the moat

One linter checks every deliverable for fabricated statistics, efficacy claims, guarantees, superlatives, and "studies show" appeals. It's a hard stop, never an auto-rewrite — a banned claim parks the run for a human. Same gate, one place, no matter which model wrote the words.

$ shaula author "promote our anxiety program"
⛔ Honesty gate stopped this draft before it shipped.
  • a "clinically proven" efficacy claim — "clinically proven to cure anxiety"
  • a percentage statistic we never publish — "95% success rate"
→ parked for human review. Nothing was sent.
What it does

Honest work, six ways

Everything below runs through the same gate, and the core never touches client data.

🛡️

Honesty gate

Unsourced stats, "proven," "guaranteed," "#1," "studies show" — caught and parked, never auto-laundered into softer copy.

🔑

Bring your own key

Google, Anthropic, or OpenAI behind one adapter seam. Swap providers in a line. Shaula ships and stores no key for you.

🗂️

Author workflows

Describe a job in plain words; get a vetted, DAG-ordered task graph whose final step is always a human review gate.

📑

Research briefs

Background briefs with every claim tagged [established] / [commonly described] / [unverified] and a source-verification checklist.

⚖️

Compliance-aware

Recommends a BAA, discloses PHI exposure, records a dated attestation — and never hard-blocks. Core functions are no-PHI by construction.

🔌

Runs offline

A deterministic --stub mode needs no key and no network. Prove the gate in CI, demo it on a plane, trust it before you pay.
